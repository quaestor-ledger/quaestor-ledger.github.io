Quaestor

A multi-tenant AR/AP ledger that observes, records, and proves — and never moves money.

Append-only ledger

Double-entry postings that must sum to zero per currency, enforced in Postgres by triggers — UPDATE and DELETE are forbidden.

Observer posture

Reads, ledgers, and reconciles across Stripe, PayPal, Braintree, Plaid, and banks — never moves money on its own license.

On-chain tamper evidence

Periodic Merkle-root anchoring to Solana with an independent third-party verification endpoint.

Read-only crypto

Watches Solana and EVM wallets into the same per-entity ledger as fiat. No keys, no spend authority.

Multi-tenant by design

Every row carries a shard key derived from tenant and regulatory region.

Two answers, fast

When do I bill the customer, and when do I pay the vendor — one GET each.
